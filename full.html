Services

Full Truckload

Kabootar is an industry leader in trucking solutions. Our Full Truckload (FTL) services support high volume and high capacity needs in a cost and time-efficient manner. With our proprietary freight platform "Orion," we connect shippers with verified and reliable fleet owners through a centralised bidding and matching engine. Our Truckload Freight (TL) platform connects shippers with fleet-owners and suppliers of truckload capacity across the country via a centralised bidding and matching engine. Our FTL service is suitable for both large enterprise clients as well as SMEs who wish to move high volume orders across India, for long and short distances. With our Full Truckload services, you can:

  • Ship high-volume orders effortlessly
  • Leverage our pan-India network of experienced trucking partners
  • Ensure pilferage-proof on time deliveries
